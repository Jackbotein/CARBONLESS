Our Mission


Over the past two centuries our world has become addicted to carbon intensive fossil fuels. These fuels have brought short term prosperity, at the expense of the future. With just 1.5 degrees celsius of warming (projected to occur between 2030-2052), the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change estimates that climate change will lead to surges in global conflict, a fifty percent spike in water insecurity, and damage to coastal regions. More troubling, by 2100, the Climate Impact Lab at the University of Chicago has estimated that climate related mortality will surpass that of all infectious diseases.

Climate change is an existential threat to humanity.

In America, our government has failed to act on climate change. Dirty industry lobbying has prevented federal climate action, meaning that individual emissions reductions are critical. Jack Botein founded Carbonless Living in the summer of 2020 to enable environmentally conscious individuals to fully eliminate their residential carbon emmisions by renting carbon neutral apartments. The apartment buildings on our platform do this by installing solar panels and purchasing carbon offset credits (ex. a forest preserve sequesters one ton of carbon dioxide, sells us the right to emit one ton of carbon). According to the National Academy of Sciences, twenty percent of American greenhouse gas emissions are created by heating, cooling and powering households (this is huge). By renting your next apartment from Carbonless, you can eliminate your entire residential carbon footprint.

Our mission is to make it easy for people to find carbon neutral apartment rentals.
